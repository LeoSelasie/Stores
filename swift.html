Brief about SWIFT

Dalex SWIFT is a smart investment account, SWIFT offers competitive rates on your investments, with an interest of up to 15% annually. This product is convenient; it allows you to make even deposit of a target amount daily, weekly, monthly, or ad hoc with as low as GHC5. Deposit from the unlimited convenience of a mobile phone. Your phone number is your account number. SWIFT invests the contributions made over 30-day period. SWIFT pays a competitive compound interest every month and provides access to your investment information at all times using your mobile phone. In addition, you can obtain a loan when you have cash flow problems without breaking your investment.

SWIFT Processes & Procedures

Deposit

1. Dial *721#
2. Select Option 1 (Deposit).
3. Select Self/Third Party.
4. Enter Amount.
5. Enter MM PIN and approve transaction

Loan

1. Dial *721#
2. Select Option 4 (Loan)
3. Select Option 1 (Apply for Loan)
4. Enter Amount.
A Dalex Agent will contact with 24 hours

Redemption

1. Dial *721#
2. Select Option 5(Redeem Investment)
3. Enter SWIFT PIN

A Dalex Agent will contact with 24 hours

Check Balance

2. Select Option 2 (My Account)
3. Select Option 1 (Check Balance)

Change PIN

3. Select Option 3 (Change PIN)

Refer A Friend

1. Dial *721#
2. Select Option 3 (Refer A Friend).
3. Enter your friend's phone number.

SWIFT FAQs

1. What is SWIFT?

SWIFT, (an acronym for Safe Wise Investments for Tomorrow) makes you confident of your future because it allows you to make regular contributions into a safe investment account.

2. Who can sign up to use the product?

Anyone who is eighteen years of age and above can sign up. Minors (people under the age of eighteen years) can be signed up by their parents/guardians in trust for them.

3. What are the details I need to provide during the sign up?

You are to provide the following personal data:
1. Phone number
2. Name
3. National ID
4. Date of Birth
5. Residential Address
6. Occupation
7. Hometown
8. Next of Kin
9. Address of Next of Kin
10. Phone number of Next of Kin
11. Security Question

4. Can I have multiple accounts?

Yes. However, each individual account will be linked to a different mobile number.

5. Can I invest for others?

Yes, provided you have their phone numbers.

6. How do I roll-over my matured funds?

• Calling our call centre on 0242435650.
• Making a request through our WhatsApp line 0556489260.

7. How long can I invest?

You can invest for 12, 24, 36, 48 or 60 months..

8. Is there any charge on my investment?

No, there is absolutely no charge on your investment.

9. How many times can I withdraw money from my SWIFT account?

SWIFT is an investment hence you cannot withdraw your investment like a savings account. However, if you decide to redeem or terminate your investment these are the terms:

10. How long before I can redeem my investment?

You are expected to redeem your investment after maturity. However, if you would like to redeem/terminate your investment before maturity, you can only do this after 3 months. This means you would receive only your principal without your interest.

11. What is the minimum amount to deposit?

The minimum contribution amount is One Ghana Cedi (GH¢1.00).

12. What is the minimum investment amount?

You start earning interest once you have at least Five Ghana Cedis (GH¢5.00) in your account

13. Is there a penalty on early termination?

No, there is no penalty on early termination before half the investment period. However, you are paid only your principal.

14. Why should I invest my money in SWIFT?

It is safe:
• Dalex Finance is a large well-established company that was voted the ‘2016 Business of the Year’ by the Ghana Economic Forum,
• Dalex Finance is regulated by the Bank of Ghana;
It is convenient:
• You can contribute as low as Five Cedis,
• You can deposit your money easily through your mobile phone by mobile money or your Visa card/Mastercard.
It pays the best rates:
• Your money earns 15% interest per annum.

15. How long do I need to invest to qualify for a loan?

You need to contribute for at least three months, ensuring that your loan request constitutes 80% or less of amount invested.

16. How much interest do I pay on a loan?

You pay a monthly interest of 2% on a loan.

17. Is there any charge (commission, processing fee) on disbursed loans?

No, there is no charge on disbursed loans.

18. How long can I repay a loan?

It cannot go beyond your investment term. Your investment acts as a collateral.

19. What happens if I have a month remaining on my investment and I need a loan?

You may need to extend your investment term or else you will be expected to repay the loan within the remaining period

20. How do I repay my SWIFT loan?

You can repay your SWIFT loan by using your mobile money wallet (MTN, AirtelTigo and Vodafone), through mobile money merchants (Mtn, AirtelTigo and Vodafone).

21. How do I sign up to SWIFT?

You can sign up using one of the following methods:
You can sign up through a Dalex SWIFT Field Sales Agent who will take all the necessary details.
You can download the SWIFT mobile application on your android smartphone (other platforms will soon be available) and register by filling in your details.
You can also log on to www.dalexswift.com to complete the registration.
You can contact a Call Centre Agent by dialing 721 (toll free) to be signed up.
