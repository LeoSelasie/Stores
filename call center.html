Business Loans

To know more about Business Loans, read the FAQs below.

BL FAQs

1. What is the BL goal?

• To create ‘Profitable Customers’ by disbursing ‘Quality Loans’ and putting the profitable customer ‘first at all times.

2. What the business loan cycle?

• Business Development
• Credit Evaluation
• Loan Administration
• Loan Recovery

3. What are the BL requirements?

• 15percent of loan amount must be invested with Dalex.
• The loan amount must be gh500,000 or more
• The collateral must be a landed property and land three times the value of the loan amount.
• The property must be in Accra
• Interest on loan is 4-6% per annum

4. How long does it Take to Process a Business loan?

A minimum of 2 weeks.

5. Can I request for a business loan even if I don’t have a collateral?

Yes, however other receivables like payment history and where the payment will be coming from will be confirmed.

6. Do I pay a fee when my business loan is processed?

Yes, you pay a processing fee.

7. What the business loan products?

• Project finance
• Vehicle finance
• Certificate/invoice discounting.
• Working capital loan.
• Bridge loan

8. What is the business loan tenor?

Not more than 24 months
