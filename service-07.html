CALL SCRIPTS

Get the script, tips for implementation, and even helpful variations.

CLIENT REQUEST PORTAL

INTRODUCTION: Good Morning/Afternoon/Evening, Sir/Madam. My name is… calling from Dalex SWIFT. Am I talking to Mr./Miss/Mrs. ……

BODY: I am calling with respect to a LOAN/REDEMPTION/PIN RESET request made on our USSD portal. To unable me process your request please help me verify your account
Verification Details
• Account Number
• ID Number
• Date of Birth
• Next of Kin
• Security Question
• Payment Number

UNCOVERING ADDITIONAL NEEDS: Mr./Miss/Mrs. … is there other issue you will need clarification on please
CONCLUSION: Payment will be made within 48hrs, we will call to confirm receipt. Thank you, Mr./Miss/Mrs., ……. for your time

INCOMING CALL SCRIPT


INTRODUCTION: Thank you for calling Dalex Finance / SWIFT Serwaa speaking how may I help you

ACKNOWLEDGE CLIENT'S INITIAL STATEMENT: Mr.…. If I understand you/ to clarify, you ….......

APPROPRIATE USE OF CLIENT'S NAME: May I know your name please

BUILDING A RAPPORT: Using the clients name least 3x throughout the conversation

EMPATHIZING: Mr./Miss/Mrs. … I apologies sincerely for your experience, I can help, kindly assist me with further information

ACTIVE LISTENING: Mr./Miss/Mrs. …., Please hold whilst I transfer Mr.…., Please hold whilst I get information for you Thank you, Mr./Miss/Mrs., …, for holding

UNCOVERING ADDITIONAL NEEDS: Mr./Miss/Mrs.…., would you need further assistance on any other issue please?

CLOSING: Thank You Mr./Miss/Mrs. … for calling Dalex finance / SWIFT have a good day.

CALL SCRIPT FOR MATURED INVESTMENT ACCOUNTS

Opening: Good Morning/Afternoon, Sir/Madam, my name is… calling from Dalex SWIFT

Body: Mr./Madam…I am calling about Your SWIFT account which is maturing on 28th ……... Roll over will be done into the next investment year for your money to continue earning interest.

Client: Yes rollover

Closing: Do you have any challenge with your account you need assistance with please? Thank you for your investing with us.

Client: Please process redemption.

Agent: Find out why and explain reasons why Dalex is the secured place for investment.

SCRIPT FOR RECOVERY CALLS

(Recent /Active Loans/Undue Maturity Period)

Good Morning Mr./Mrs./Miss Akoto, how are you doing? MY Name is …………………... speaking from Dalex Finance. I called in relation to a loan you have with us of which we not receiving deductions. Sir/Madam, please are you aware about that and do you have any idea why the deductions are not coming?

If YES and will like to make payment……… Sir/Madam, you can make payment using our Mobile Money Account or GCB Account (GHC 50 and Above) depending on your preferred choice. After payment, kindly call to inform us for payment confirmation and tracking purpose. We will also call you at the end of every month to remind you of the payment.

If YES/NO and unaware…... Sir/Madam, we will like to resubmit the loan for deduction to resume on your pay slip. Kindly assist with your password to enable us to resubmit the loan for deductions to resume. (Please note that due to this, the loan tenure or duration will be extended by the number of months deductions were missed.)

If YES and have Validation issue /Expect to be reinstated ………... Sir/Madam, we will confirm if deductions resume for subsequent month and if deduction does not resume, we will call back for further assistance.

Thank you, Sir/Madam.
